# Dubai Investment Strategy

Dubai rewards long‑term, fundamentals‑driven strategy. Off‑plan offers the strongest return potential, while ready property provides stability and immediate yield.

## Off‑Plan Strategy (Highest Return Potential)

Off‑plan delivers the strongest returns due to:

- early‑phase pricing  
- flexible payment plans  
- leverage  
- developer incentives  
- long‑term appreciation  

But it requires:

- developer reliability  
- cycle awareness  
- community evaluation  
- payment plan risk management

## Cashflow & Exit Strategy (Deep Dive)

This section contains more detailed strategy guidance. Expand if you want the full breakdown.

<Drawer title="Cashflow Management & Exit Strategy (Full Breakdown)">

### Cashflow Management

Investors must plan:

- stage payments  
- service charges  
- mortgage instalments  
- rental income timing  
- maintenance reserves  

Cashflow determines whether an investor can hold through cycle changes.

### Exit Strategy

Exit strategy depends on:

- cycle phase  
- community maturity  
- rental yield trajectory  
- resale liquidity  
- assignment market conditions  

Exit planning is essential for off‑plan investors who may resell before handover.
