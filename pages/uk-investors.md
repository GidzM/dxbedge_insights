## Exchange‑Rate Advantage

AED is pegged to USD.  
Sterling fluctuations create:

- increased purchasing power  
- lower effective acquisition cost  
- higher rental yield in GBP  
- dual‑return potential (property + currency)

Expand below for full examples.

<Drawer title="Exchange‑Rate Examples for UK Investors">

### Increased Purchasing Power

A property costs AED 5,000,000.

| Exchange Rate | Cost in GBP |
|---------------|-------------|
| £1 = AED 4.50 | £1,111,111  |
| £1 = AED 5.00 | £1,000,000  |

Sterling strength reduces acquisition cost by £111,000.

### Rental Income Advantage

Annual rent = AED 240,000.

| Rate | GBP Income |
|------|------------|
| £1 = AED 4.50 | £53,333 |
| £1 = AED 5.20 | £46,154 |

Currency timing affects effective yield.

### Dual Returns

Property appreciation + currency movement = amplified returns.
